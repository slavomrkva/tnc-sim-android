Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 July 2026

TNC Sim ("the app") is a free, browser-based CNC simulator for Heidenhain TNC programming, available at tncsim.org and as an Android application. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

Summary

TNC Sim does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Everything you do runs locally in your browser or on your device.

Data we collect

We do not collect personal data. The app has no user accounts, no login, and does not ask for your name, email, or any identifying information.

Programs and content you create

Any CNC program you write or import stays on your own device (in your browser or the app). It is not uploaded to any server. If you export a program, the file is saved locally by you.

Local storage

The app may store small amounts of data locally on your device (for example your theme preference or lesson progress) so the app works better on your next visit. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser or app data.

Offline use

The Android app and the website use a service worker to cache files so the app works offline. These cached files are stored only on your device.

Analytics and tracking

The app itself contains no advertising and no third-party tracking. The website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages, which may process standard server request logs (such as IP address and browser type) for security and reliability, as described in Cloudflare's own privacy policy. We do not use this data to identify individuals.

Bug reports

If you choose to send a bug report, the app may pre-fill a message with basic technical details (such as your browser type and screen size) to help diagnose the issue. Sending it is entirely your choice, and no report is sent automatically.

Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact info@tncsim.org.
